# ClipForge Vision

## One-Liner
AI that turns any long video into viral-ready short clips — like an autopilot for content repurposing.

## Why
Creators spend hours cutting, subtitling, and reformatting content. ClipForge automates the entire pipeline: import → analyze → clip → subtitle → crop → export.

## Target User
- Podcasters repurposing episodes into Shorts/Reels/TikToks
- Streamers clipping highlights automatically
- Educational creators extracting key moments
- Social media managers batch-processing content

## North Star
One button: paste link, get 5 viral-ready clips with subtitles, face tracking, and smart crop.

## Principles
1. **Zero config** — paste URL, get clips. No sliders, no thresholds.
2. **AI-native** — LLMs choose what's interesting, not energy heuristics.
3. **Export quality** — 1080p, GPU-accelerated, watermark-free.
4. **SaaS-ready** — billing, auth, multi-tenant from day one.

## Future
- Real-time livestream clipping
- Multi-language subtitle translation
- Auto title/description/hashtag generation
- Direct upload to TikTok/Reels/Shorts API
- Team collaboration workspaces
- Template-based brand presets
